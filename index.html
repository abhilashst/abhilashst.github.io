About Me

What makes my clock tick?

I come from a rural background where, in my childhood, we didn’t have much access to technology. I started to question myself about how we heard sound from the ‘magic box’ – radio or how a TV worked. With each answered question my thoughts became clearer and there was no holding me back. I had found my passion in technology. Since then I was exploring new technologies. The constant thirst to learn something new and to apply it in day-to-day situations has always been my motivation.

Technology is the game-changer in the era that we live right now. People have become more and more dependent on technology and I’ve taken it upon myself to make sure that people are given access to technology that will change their lives for the better. I’m passionate about exploring technology and innovations.
